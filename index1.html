Spell-Hacks is a student-led hackathon hosted by Google Developer Group on Campus, UIT RGPV Bhopal, and powered by Hack2skill.

This hackathon is all about solving real problems around you - on campus or in your local community - by building practical solutions using Google technologies. It's a space to learn by doing, collaborate with peers, and turn ideas into meaningful projects.

Build for Campus & Community

Identify everyday challenges students or locals face. Improve services, safety, or communication. Solve what you see.

Google Technologies

Leverage Google technologies and AI tools. Whether it's Flutter, Firebase, or TensorFlow, build practical solutions.

Innovation & Impact

Emphasize innovation, practicality, and measurable real-world impact. Even small improvements can create big positive change.

"Improve what matters. Build for the people around you."
